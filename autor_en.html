About the Author

Marjan Mitrović (1979) is a writer and football referee, currently living in Prague. He actively engages with themes of labor, class struggle, and social relations. “New Slavery” is his debut novel, in which he combines personal experience of work with a broader reflection on the capitalist system. His ambition is to use literature as a space for dialogue and critical thought about the condition of workers today.
